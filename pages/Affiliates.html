As a way to reach our ultimate goal, we actively seek to cooperate and establish relationships with many groups and companies around the world.

True to its name, BlackSteel Worldwide is not an ordinary PMC, but one that is armed with both modern armament and modern thoughts. Founded in Columbia by Rupert “Clip” Cliff in 1026 after the Columbian War of Independence, its HQ is located in their signature state-of-the-art landship, “Fort Barron”, witch provides mobile administration, logistic and supply center and professional personel training. BlackSteel also offers services of disaster rescue and critical emergency response and poses a specialiced unid, The Biochemical Protection Response Staff or BPRS, that is tasked to guarantee the safety of all personel and prevent or contain the oripathic infections.

Founded in 1083 by Dr. Saria and Kristen Wrigth with the purpose of seeking scientific advancement and discover whats beyond the charted lands. Rhine Labs eventually became one of Terra's biggest scientific organizations, famous throughout Terra for their Originium-related research and advancements in robotics, medicine and ecological studies.

Karlan Trade Company, also know as Karlan Commercial, was founded by Enciodes Silverash, the current patriarch of the Silverash clan, in the year 1090 after staying aboard in Victoria for more than twenty years. With the aid of Ratatos Browntail, the matriarch of the Browntail clan, Enciodes was able to persuade the Great Elder into opening up the country through his company. As a way to reform and modernize the country, Karlan Trade acts as the only bridge between Kjerag and the outside world, mainly participating in economic activities and technological trade.

The Maylander Foundation, also know as Maylander for short, is an NGO founded by Selene Maylander in Columbia after the declaration of independence of the country, it possesses expansive ororganizations and sub-branches under its name, and its broad influence can be seen in many fields such as charity, education, enviromental protection and public policies all working under they motto of maintaining the well being of all columbian citizens.
